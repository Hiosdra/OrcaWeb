How it works

Simple three-step workflow

Upload, configure, slice. No installation required.

1

Upload STL

Drag & drop your STL file. A 3D preview appears instantly on the virtual print bed.

2

Configure

Choose printer, material, and quality preset. Import your OrcaSlicer profiles for exact settings.

3

Slice

Click Slice. OrcaSlicer WASM runs locally in a Web Worker and returns G-code in milliseconds.

4

Inspect & download

Browse layers in the G-code visualiser, then download the file ready for your printer.
